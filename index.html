Vocabulary Tests

The 120 assigned words are divided into 3 word lists, to help you to space out your learning. A page number is given after each word. Your first task is to locate these words in your course book to see how they are used in context.

Test 1 (5%) – Week 5, Lesson 2 (3, 4 or 5 October)

  • The 40 words in Vocabulary List 1 will be tested.
  • There are 20 fill-in-the-blank sentences. The words will be provided for you.
  • You have 15 minutes to complete the test. Your instructor will let you know whether the test will be given at the beginning or at the end of the lesson.

Test 2 (10%) – Week 12, Lesson 1 (19, 20 or 21 November)

  • All 120 assigned words will be tested.
  • There are 40 fill-in-the-blank sentences. The words will be provided for you.
  • You have 30 minutes to complete the test. Your instructor will let you know whether the test will be given at the beginning or at the end of the lesson.

Important notes on the tests

  • You will take the test under exam conditions: no notes, no dictionaries, no talking.
  • Each word can be used only once.
  • You may need to change the form of the word.
  • You will get no marks for the wrong spelling.
